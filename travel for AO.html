Organization for Travel

You can use a tote to store important things like your money, passport or phone.


Use a plastic bag to put lotions, shampoos, makeup in so that they don't spill.


Put all items from traveling in a box for keepsake so that it is organized based on different trips.


Cut a cork board to represent the United States or the country and put a pin or a photo of where you have been.



Use a pill container to organize earrings, necklaces or rings.
